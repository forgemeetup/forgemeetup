Who we are

Forge is a meetup community for anyone involved or interested in how lawyers in tech partner with and innovate fast moving businesses - a place to share experiences, knowledge and hear from the biggest players in the industry. A Forge meetup is a travelling event where the focus will rotate around themes like privacy and commercial advice.
